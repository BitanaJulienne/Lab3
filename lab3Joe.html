Joe Jonas is the lead singer of the band. He is mostly known as a member of “Jonas Brothers,” his former band along with him and his brothers, Nick and Kevin. He is currently in a relationship with Sophie Turner, the Game of Thrones actress. Joe began his career at age 12 when he was cast in Baz Luhrmann's production of La Bohème on Broadway. He was also able to work with the Disney company. Joe is one simple guy who loves Red Velvet cake.
